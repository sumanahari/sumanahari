PUBLICATIONS
  • Progressively Balanced Multi-class Neural Trees

    Twenty Fourth National Conference on Communications (NCC) 2018

A progressively balanced neural tree has been proposed where training datasets are balanced prior to perceptron learning. The decision function has been optimized with respect to entropy impurity based objective functions. This formulation also allows a parent node to have more than two child nodes. The proposed algorithm has been benchmarked on ten standard datasets against three baseline multi-class classification algorithms.
